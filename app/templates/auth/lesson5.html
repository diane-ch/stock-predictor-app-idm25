Debt-to-Equity - The Stress Test ⚖️

This shows how much debt a company has vs. its own money.
Healthy range: 0.1 to 0.4 (company owns most of its assets) Yellow flag: 0.5 to 0.8 (manageable but watch closely)
Red flag: Above 1.0 (more debt than equity - risky!)
Banks and real estate can handle more debt than tech companies
